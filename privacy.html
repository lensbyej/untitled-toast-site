Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 7, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Untitled Toast ("we," "our," or "us") handles information across all applications, software, and services published under the Untitled Toast name (collectively, the "Apps").

We are committed to protecting user privacy and designing our Apps with data minimization as a core principle.

1. Scope

This Privacy Policy applies to all current and future Apps developed or distributed by Untitled Toast, unless a specific App provides a separate privacy policy that supersedes this document.

2. Information Collection

2.1 Personal Information

By default, our Apps do not require user accounts and do not collect personal information such as:

  • Names
  • Email addresses
  • Phone numbers
  • Physical addresses
  • Government-issued identifiers

If an App ever requires personal information to function, it will be clearly disclosed within that App and, where required, through an updated privacy policy.

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

Our Apps do not collect:

  • Usage analytics
  • Advertising identifiers
  • Device identifiers
  • Location data
  • Behavioral tracking data

We do not use cookies, tracking pixels, or third-party advertising technologies unless explicitly stated otherwise.

3. Device Permissions

Some Apps may request device permissions strictly to enable core features. These may include, but are not limited to:

  • Camera access
  • Photo or media library access
  • Local storage access
  • Microphone access

All permissions are:

  • Requested only when necessary
  • Used only for user-initiated actions
  • Limited to on-device functionality unless explicitly stated otherwise

4. Data Storage and Processing

Unless explicitly disclosed within a specific App:

  • All data is stored locally on the user’s device
  • Data is processed on-device
  • Data is not transmitted to Untitled Toast servers
  • Untitled Toast does not have access to user-generated content

Users remain in full control of their data, including deletion through standard device or App controls.

5. Third-Party Services

Our Apps do not integrate third-party services that collect user data by default.

If a specific App uses third-party services (such as cloud storage, analytics, or external APIs), such usage will be clearly disclosed within that App, including the nature of data shared and the purpose of the integration.

6. Children’s Privacy

Our Apps are not designed to knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13.

If you believe that a child has provided personal information through one of our Apps, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.

7. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect data handled by our Apps. Since most data remains on-device, security is primarily governed by the user’s device operating system and security settings.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected by an updated "Last updated" date.

Continued use of our Apps after changes are made constitutes acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.

9. Contact Information

If you have questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or our privacy practices, you may contact us at: legal@untitledtoast.com
